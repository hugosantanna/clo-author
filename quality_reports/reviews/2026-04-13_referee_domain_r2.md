## Remaining Concerns

### 1. Causal language not reframed
Paper still says "efecto causal del gasto" without qualifying that identification is conditional on CRE assumption. Text-only fix required.
**What would change my mind:** 2–3 sentences in §3 and conclusion: estimates are conditional on the CRE assumption of no time-varying unobserved heterogeneity; no excluded instrument was available; Oster bounds validate robustness to static OVB.

### 2. Kinnaman (2006) benchmark
Kinnaman (2006) concerns US curbside recycling, not developing-country landfill programs.
**What would change my mind:** Replace or supplement with Hoornweg and Bhada-Tata (2012) "What a Waste" (World Bank).

### 3. QPRS amplification asymmetry
One sentence explaining why QPRS amplification is ~4x larger than PI amplification.
**What would change my mind:** One sentence — e.g., whether QPRS captures a floor effect, threshold dynamics, or differential measurement error.

### 4. JEL codes
Confirm O18 and H77 appear on title page. Trivial fix.
